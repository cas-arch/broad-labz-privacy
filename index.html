Privacy Policy

Size Advisor Pro — Broad Labz
Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Size Advisor Pro collects anonymous measurement data (height, weight) entered by shoppers when using the size advisor widget. This data is associated with the merchant's shop domain, not with individual customer identities.

2. How We Use Information

Collected data is used solely to provide size recommendations and analytics to merchants. We do not sell, share, or transfer this data to third parties.

3. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. Merchants may request deletion of their data at any time by uninstalling the app or contacting us.

4. GDPR Compliance

We comply with Shopify's mandatory data privacy requirements. Upon receiving a shop/redact or customer/redact webhook, all associated data is deleted from our systems within 48 hours.

5. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact us at: privacy@broadlabz.com
